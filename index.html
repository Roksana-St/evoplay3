About the Game

Farm Sort Puzzle is a relaxing and challenging puzzle game where players must sort and organize items on a vibrant farm. The goal is to clear each level by sorting items into their correct locations. As you progress, puzzles become more difficult, introducing new farm-related items and obstacles that require careful planning and strategic thinking. Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts and casual gamers alike, Farm Sort Puzzle offers an engaging experience for all ages.
