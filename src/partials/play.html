How to Play

  • Move items to their proper locations in each level by following the game's instructions.

    Sort the Items

  • Successfully match and organize the items to clear the puzzle within a set number of moves.

    Complete the Puzzle

  • Unlock rewards and new levels by collecting stars from completed puzzles.

    Earn Stars

  • As the game progresses, face increasingly difficult puzzles and obstacles that require strategic sorting and planning.

    Progress and Challenge Yourself
